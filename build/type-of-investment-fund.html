Type of Investment Fund

Blue Penny Property Development Fund (BPPDF) Structure:

BPPDF will operate as a mutual fund and therefore the Protected Cell Company (PCC) shall be set up under Companies Act 1972.

Protected Cell Company (PCC):

Assets are segregated into different cells within that company under a PCC structure. Each cell is protected from any extension of liabilities from one cell to the other. The structure consists of one board who will oversee the entire structure and an advisory committee to each cell. It is also a cost efficient structure with one administrator, one audit firm and one company secretary.
